WORLD OF MARIA CLARA & IBARRA

Maria Clara at Ibarra is a TV series based on the famous Filipino novel "Noli Me Tangere" by José Rizal.

The story is set in the Philippines during the Spanish colonial period and revolves around Maria Clara, a beautiful and kind woman, and Crisostomo Ibarra, a young man who returns to the Philippines after studying abroad. The series tells the story of their love and the struggles they face in a society filled with injustice. In the GMA Teleserye version, "Maria Clara at Ibarra"brings the classic story to life in a way that is easy to understand for modern viewers. The show combines the past with today’s world, making it exciting and relatable. It shows the challenges and dreams of the characters, as well as the problems they face because of the government and social systems at the time.

Directed by Zig Dulay, it stars Barbie Forteza, Julie Anne San Jose and Dennis Trillo. It follows Klay Infantes, a Gen-Z nursing student who gets transported into the setting of Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo.

AIRING TIME

"Maria Clara at Ibarra" aired on GMA Telebabad from October 3, 2022 to February 10, 2023, at 8:00 PM Philippine Standard Time (PST).
The series had a total of 105 episodes.

RATINGS

According to AGB Nielsen Philippines' Nationwide Urban Television Audience Measurement People in television homes,
the pilot episode of "Maria Clara at Ibarra" earned a 15.1% rating. The final episode scored a 14.3% rating.
